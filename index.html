How it works
Generate a week, review it, then save it. The shopping list updates from the meals on the plan. Tuesday is always takeout. Monday favors quicker meals or slow-cooker meals. Saturday and Sunday favor bigger meals.
Install on iPhone
Open this file in Safari, tap the Share button, then choose Add to Home Screen. It will act like a simple app and keep your saved week on the phone.
